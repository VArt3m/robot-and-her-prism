Ray flow-bulge — preview

The ray swells a couple of pixels wider where a crest passes; the swell glides from the stronger source toward the weaker end.

Top: a normal ray — one stream of swells running source → end. Middle: a same-colour tug — two beams full length, swells crossing along the centreline. Bottom: a different-colour clash — each colour's swells run in to the midpoint.

Tunables live in renderer2d.js: FLOW_BULGE (extra width), FLOW_SPEED, FLOW_LAMBDA, FLOW_SIGMA, FLOW_GLOW.
